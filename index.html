ABOUT

Security Executive Enterprises is a new security company that offers event security, personal/corporate protection, surveillance, special event planning, and paper service. Our team is comprised of active and retired law enforcement professionals with extensive security and investigative experience.

Event security experience ranges from short personal security assignments to week long events. For the past 25 years, members of our team have managed security at the Walworth County Fair. In addition to the fair, we have also managed security at the Walworth County Rib Fest, Burlington Chocolate Fest, as well as provided security for a private vendor at Country Thunder.
